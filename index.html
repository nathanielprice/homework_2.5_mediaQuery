About Me

I am the Learning Management System Manager at the Association for Energy Affordability, Inc. Outside of the responsibilities of running our LMS, I also create curriculum, which includes diagrams and images that convey difficult concepts. I use Sketchup and Adobe Illustrator to create these images. As a result of years of working on images, I consider my self to be a Sketchup Ninja! I have produced over a 1000 images that we use in our PowerPoint presentations. I have also have images published in our Multifamily Resource Guide.

As the Learning Management Systems Manager at AEA my eyes were opened to the world of coding. At one point in time in the not so distant past, we operated a open source LMS developed by the Department of Energy. This platform was written in the PHP coding language and I needed to become acquainted with the way that it worked. Once the funding was removed for this platform, the responsibility for its operation fell into my lap. It was a daunting task, but I was able to rebrand the LMS to replicate our website. This planted the coding seed deep into my brain and it is the reason I am here. I am excited to be expanding my horizons by participating in this Coding Boot-camp.
